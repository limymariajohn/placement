About Us

The Placement Cell of St. Joseph College of Engineering&Technology, Palai creates a platform where students can showcase their talents which different companies seek to explore and utilise. Starting from 2003, we have successfully placed a large section of graduates who have appeared for placements. This year especially, the performance has been overwhelming. The Placement Cell is headed by Shebin- SJ, and the coordinator. The Cell is led by the Placement officer Mr. Shebin along with the Placement Advisory Board which consists of Professors and final year students from various departments. Initially the companies are invited to take part in the recruitment drive. Those interested respond and thus register with us for the year for recruitment. The students are intimated and a vast number register for the interview. The interview process usually starts with a Power Point Presentation where the company enlightens the students about their company profile. Those interested then go for the interview, which usually comprises of a written test followed by group discussion and personal interview. The job scenario today is facing the onslaught of ground breaking competition from all fields; and employment into good companies and organizations has become a daunting task. So, for the benefit of its students preparing to face the job market, the St. Joseph College of Engineering &Technology Placement Cell has risen to the occasion and has successfully aided in providing opportunities and employment to its outgoing students. With the relentless support of the staff and the students associated with the placement cell and most significantly Shebin, S.J. at the helm, we strive to reach a step closer to our ultimate goal of 100% placements for our students.
